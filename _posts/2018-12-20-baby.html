The two of us traded positions on opposite sides of whether to have a kid a couple of times. Now we are both excited to see the world through our future child's eyes.

For Mike, he had a bit of an epiphany when taking his grandma to a doctor's appointment, repeating things to her in his loud grandma voice to make sure she knew what was going on and sitting in waiting rooms with her, and he thought - this is on its face a really annoying thing to do, but somehow, I am not annoyed because I get to be here with my grandma, and I know how important that is to her. So taking care of friends and family seemed exactly like what he should be doing in this next chapter.

We are due April 5. Here's a sonogram from a while back, conveniently annotated by the ultrasound tech.
